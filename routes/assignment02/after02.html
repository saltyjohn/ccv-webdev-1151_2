You and I have never met, kind stranger on the internet;

our lives have grown in separate ways although we share years, months, weeks, days of passing time -

as all who breathe delight from this fine earth do so, together yet apart from end to starting, birth running into death constrained by momentary choices.

confined upon a chariot of spinning stone glazed lightly verdant (trees sprouting upwards, soil washing down) until seasons dictate unbiquitous chill- prompted change.
